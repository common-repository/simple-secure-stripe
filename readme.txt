== Description ==
Simple and secure way to accept Credit Cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, SEPA, and more!

= Official Stripe Partner =
Simple & Secure WP is an official partner of Stripe.

= Protect your checkout and earn more revenue =
Seamlessly boost your revenue with our user-friendly and secure Stripe integration for WooCommerce, simplifying payments, enhancing customer experience, and empowering your WordPress store.

Simple Secure Stripe is designed to significantly increase your conversion rate by reducing payment obstacles for your customers.
Use Google Pay, Apple Pay, etc on product pages, cart pages, and at the top of your checkout page.

= Payment Options =
- Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, JCB, and Diners Club
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Afterpay, Affirm, Klarna
- ACH Payments
- Local payment methods like Konbini, PayNow, BLIK, P24, IDEAL and many more

== Features ==
- Easy to use settings
- 3DS 2.0
- WooCommerce Subscriptions
- WooCommerce Pre-Orders
- WooCommerce Blocks
- Integrates with [CheckoutWC](https://www.checkoutwc.com)
- Integrates with [Cart Flows](https://wordpress.org/plugins/cartflows/)
- Integrates with [FunnelKit](https://funnelkit.com/)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How do I test this plugin? =
Enable test mode in settings. This will allow you to try out all the features with test transactions.

= Does your plugin support WooCommerce Subscriptions? =
Of course! All functionality related to WooCommerce Subscriptions is supported.

= Where can I find additional documentation? =
We have a growing knowledgebase with technical documentation here: https://simplesecurewp.com/kb/stripe

= Does your plugin rely on 3rd-party assets? =
Because this plugin is designed to integrate with Stripe, we do leverage the [Stripe JS library](https://stripe.com/docs/js) ([source](https://js.stripe.com/v3/)) for prebuilt UI components that power [Stripe Elements](https://stripe.com/payments/elements). As always, it is a good idea when using a 3rd-party to look at their [privacy policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).
